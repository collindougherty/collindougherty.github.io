Biography

Collin Dougherty is a M.S. Math student at the University of Nebraska-Omaha and works at the University of Nebraska Medical Center in Omaha, NE as a researcher and data analyst for Surgical Oncology. He holds degrees in mathematics and psychology from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. He is particularly interested in how data can be used in a wide variety of fields, including machine learning, sports, markets, and scientific research. He interned in the field of sports analytics with the University of Nebraska and writes in his free time at huskerstats.com.
